annameredithmusic

By: Anna Cheng

Anna Meredith Music is a music page that showcases original songs I've written and covered in a stylish and personal way.

Technologies used on this webpage: vanilla JavaScript, HTML5 and CSS3.

User is able to choose a song of their choice, and the audio player will play the respective selection. Vanilla JavaScript is used on the audio button where the selected song will play on click.

The is application is designed using CSS3 and responsive flexbox layouts.
